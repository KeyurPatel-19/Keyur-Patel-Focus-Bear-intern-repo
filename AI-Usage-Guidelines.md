# Use of AI Tools

## Research & Learn

### What AI tools are typically used for your role?

In my role, AI tools like ChatGPT, claude ai and coding assistants can help with writing code, explaining technical concepts, improving documentation, and generating ideas for solving problems.

### What are the benefits and risks of using AI in a professional setting?

The main benefit of AI tools is that they can help save time and improve productivity by providing suggestions and helping with tasks. However, the risks include incorrect information, over-reliance on AI, and accidentally sharing sensitive data.

### What types of information should never be entered into AI tools?

Confidential company information, passwords, private user data, internal documents, or any sensitive business information should never be entered into AI tools.

### How can you fact-check and validate AI-generated content to ensure accuracy?

AI-generated content should always be reviewed carefully. I can check official documentation, verify information from reliable sources, and test any code before using it to make sure it is correct.

## Reflection

### When should you use AI for assistance, and when should you rely on your own skills?

I can use AI for help when learning new concepts, generating ideas, or improving writing and code. However, I should rely on my own skills when making final decisions, solving problems, and verifying the final work.

### How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it?

I can avoid over-reliance on AI by using it as a support tool rather than depending on it completely. I will review the suggestions carefully and make sure I understand the solution instead of copying it directly.

### What steps will you take to ensure data privacy when using AI tools?

To protect data privacy, I will avoid sharing confidential company information with AI tools, only use AI for general assistance, and follow company security policies.
