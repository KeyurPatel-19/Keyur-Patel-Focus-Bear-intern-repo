# Company Overview & Reflection Assignments

### Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

I think Focus Bear was created to help people who struggle with focus, distractions, and maintaining daily routines. Many people, especially those with ADHD or executive functioning challenges, find it difficult to stay on task, manage their time, or build consistent habits. Focus Bear seems to have been created to support those people by providing structure, habit guidance, and tools that reduce distractions.

### What problem is Focus Bear solving?

Focus Bear is solving the problem of digital distraction and difficulty with productivity. Many people want to focus on their work or studies but get distracted by social media, websites, or other apps. For people with ADHD or similar challenges, this can be even harder. Focus Bear helps by blocking distractions, guiding routines, and encouraging healthy work habits so users can stay focused and complete tasks.

### Why do you think this mission is important?

This mission is important because many students and professionals struggle with productivity and mental overload in a world full of digital distractions. When people cannot focus well, it can affect their academic performance, work performance, and even their confidence. Tools like Focus Bear can help people stay organised, build positive habits, and feel more in control of their daily lives.
