## Then, reflect on the following questions:

### What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?

After going through the apps listed in the task such as Freedom, Cold Turkey, Tiimo, and Routinery, I noticed that Focus Bear is different because it combines multiple productivity features in one place. While Freedom and Cold Turkey mainly focus on blocking distracting websites and apps, and Tiimo and Routinery focus more on routines and planning, Focus Bear tries to support the whole productivity process. It helps users build routines, block distractions, and stay focused while also supporting people who struggle with attention and consistency.

### If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?

If I were a user, I would choose Focus Bear because it feels more complete compared to the other apps I explored. Instead of using separate apps for focus blocking and routine management, Focus Bear combines these features together. I also feel that Focus Bear is designed in a way that understands the real challenges people face with focus and productivity, especially for people who struggle with distractions.

### What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?

One feature that some other apps like Tiimo provide is a more visual daily planning system. It allows users to see their daily schedule in a timeline format, which can make planning tasks easier for some users.

### Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?

Based on my research after going through these apps, one improvement Focus Bear could make is adding a stronger visual daily planner. This could help users organize their tasks and routines more clearly during the day. Even though Focus Bear already provides strong focus and habit-building tools, adding visual planning features could make the app even more helpful.
